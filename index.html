Fantasy World you waited for.

Waka provides a huge set of common tools, so you can just focus on making your game without reinventing the wheel.

Waka is completely free and open-source under the very permissive MIT license. No strings attached, no royalties, nothing. Your world is yours, down to the last line of engine code.
